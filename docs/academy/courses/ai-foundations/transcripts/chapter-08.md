The four trust trip-wires. *Hard nos.*

*One — no personal data to free consumer tools.* Use the enterprise version your company licenses. If you don't have one, ask IT.

*Two — no AI-generated specific facts that you haven't verified.* Names, dates, statistics, citations. *Verify against the primary source.*

*Three — no AI signing anything binding.* If the output is a customer commitment, a legal statement, a policy decision — a human signs it.

*Four — no "set and forget."* Track what works. Track what doesn't. *Adjust monthly.*
