*Quarter two — one of routine accruals, journal entry AI, or AP automation.* Pick by which sub-function in your finance team has the cleanest source data. *Apply the materiality-threshold filter from chapter four.*

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · trust trip-wires]

Four trust trip-wires. *Hard nos.*

*One — no AI signs the close.* Controller and CFO sign. AI is an assistant. *Never the signer.*

*Two — no unreviewed accrual posts to the GL.* AI proposes. Human approves. *No exceptions, even on routine accruals.*

*Three — no untraceable source data.* Every AI-touched entry links to a source document from an approved system. *No source. No entry.*

*Four — no "set and forget."* Quarterly accuracy review. Quarterly audit committee disclosure. *Stop measuring, you've lost the controls.*
