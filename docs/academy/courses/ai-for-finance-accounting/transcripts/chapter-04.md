The Big-Four acceptance criteria. *PwC, Deloitte, KPMG, EY converged twenty twenty-four to twenty twenty-five guidance.* Three conditions all must hold. *One — underlying source data is traceable.* Every AI-proposed JE links back to a specific source document. > [S5 ▸ reveal 1 · quad-eye]

The quad-eye flow. *Standard in mature deployments by twenty twenty-five.*

*Drafter — AI.* Generates the proposed JE from the source document.

*Preparer — analyst.* Reviews. Confirms calculation. Adjusts if needed. Signs off.

*Reviewer — controller or manager.* Independent review. Checks materiality, account coding, period. Signs off.

*Approver — director or CFO for material entries.* Final approval before posting.

The discipline. *Each role is a distinct person* with a distinct sign-off. *Same person doing preparer and reviewer breaks the segregation-of-duties control.*
