The failure modes. *Hallucinations in clinical AI are patient safety risks.* The Stanford "Hallucinating Law" pattern applies — *if AI is making up details in legal briefs, it can make up details in clinical documentation.* That's a medical-error vector.

The Air Canada Moffatt cross-domain. *Anything the AI says to a patient becomes the hospital's statement.* For patient-facing AI — coverage commitments, treatment recommendations, appointment promises — the hospital is responsible.
