> [S2 ▸ reveal 1 · 9 questions]

Nine questions in three categories.

*Category one — governance (3 questions).*

*Q1.* Who is the named accountable executive for each high-risk AI system? *Maturity answer: specific names. Rehearsal answer: "the team."*

*Q2.* What policies govern AI use? When were they last updated? *Maturity: recent specific. Rehearsal: "we have policies."*

*Q3.* How does AI risk appear in board reporting? *Maturity: shown in last 3 board decks. Rehearsal: "we discuss it."*

*Category two — operations (3 questions).*

*Q4.* For our top AI system, what's the eval suite and how often does it run? *Maturity: specific gold cases + cadence. Rehearsal: "we evaluate."*

*Q5.* What happens when the AI is wrong? *Maturity: documented incident playbook with named owners. Rehearsal: "we'd respond."*

*Q6.* How do we know when the model behaviour has drifted? *Maturity: specific monitoring. Rehearsal: "we'd notice."*

*Category three — assurance (3 questions).*

*Q7.* Which AI controls has internal audit tested in the last 12 months? *Maturity: specific list. Rehearsal: "we'll get to AI."*

*Q8.* What external assurance covers AI? *Maturity: specific (SOC report scope, regulatory exam findings). Rehearsal: "we're regulated."*

*Q9.* What would make you change the AI risk rating? *Maturity: specific signals + thresholds. Rehearsal: "we'd reassess."*

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · maturity vs rehearsal]

Distinguishing maturity from rehearsal.

*Maturity answer characteristics.* Specific. Includes names. References evidence (date, document, finding). Acknowledges current weakness.

*Rehearsal answer characteristics.* General. Future-tense framing. Generic best-practice language. No acknowledged gaps.

The committee's job. *Press for specifics. When you get generality, ask one more time. Three rounds of generality = rehearsal.*

How to score audit committee inquiry discipline. *Two questions.*

*Question one — has the committee walked all 9 questions with the AI risk owner in the last 12 months?* Yes documented green · informally amber · *no red — questions haven't been asked*.

*Question two — when answers were generic, did the committee press for specifics or accept?* Pressed green · sometimes amber · *accepted red — committee theater*.
