> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · 4 trip-wires]

Four trust trip-wires. *Do not cross.*

*Trip-wire one — buying a platform without fixing the data.* Modern platforms don't fix bad data. *They store it more expensively.*

*Trip-wire two — ocean-boiling cleanup projects.* Six-month cleanup with no consumable outcome. *The data product pattern delivers value early and compounds.*

*Trip-wire three — data team disconnected from business.* Engineers building what they think the business needs. *Co-locate. Embed. Outcome-driven funding.*

*Trip-wire four — privacy bolted on.* Privacy patterns at design time, not retrofit. *GDPR right-to-deletion that doesn't work is a regulator letter waiting to happen.*
