> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 3-metric structure]

The three-metric structure that survives a full board cycle.

*Metric one — AI value realised vs projected.* From business cases approved in last 12 months, how much of projected ROI has materialised? *Honest. Comparable to other capital programs.*

*Metric two — AI delivery confidence.* For features in-flight, on-track / at-risk / behind. *Same structure as ERP modernization or cloud migration.*

*Metric three — AI portfolio health.* Spend by feature, vendor concentration, lock-in exposure, talent capacity. *Strategic risk metrics the board cares about.*

Three metrics. *Not 12 dashboards.* Boards remember three.
