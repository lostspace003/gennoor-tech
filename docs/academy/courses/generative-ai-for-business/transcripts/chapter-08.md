*Month two — measure and adjust.* From chapter five. Baseline. Counterfactual. Quality. *Active-user fraction.* Identify what's working, what's not. Adjust the workflow, the prompts, the team support.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:55]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · trust trip-wires]

Four trust trip-wires. *Hard nos.*

*One — no untested AI in customer-facing or regulated work.* Until you've measured the failure mode, customer-facing AI is a reputational risk.

*Two — no personal or sensitive data to free consumer tools.* Enterprise terms required. *Training opt-out. Tenant isolation.*

*Three — no AI signing material commitments.* Customer contracts. Legal statements. Policy decisions. *A human signs.*

*Four — no "set and forget."* Monthly review of the measurement framework. *Adjust based on data, not opinion.*
