## Thesis

Microsoft 365 Copilot has gone from "expensive license that goes unused" to the largest single AI productivity bet for enterprises by 2026. The companies that are extracting real value from Copilot have one thing in common: they treat it as an adoption-and-change-management challenge, not a licensing or technology challenge. This course is the playbook for IT leaders + change managers running Copilot rollouts at scale.

## Chapter blueprint

### Chapter 1 — The M365 Copilot landscape (~6 min)
**What:** Microsoft 365 Copilot GA Nov 2023. Pricing $30/user/month enterprise. Work Trend Index ~30% time savings for active users. The active-user gap (analyst surveys 30-40%). Thesis: adoption is the challenge.
**Sources:** [1], [2], [3], [4], [18], [19].

### Chapter 2 — Where Copilot delivers value (~5 min)
**What:** Word drafting + outlining. Excel analysis + formula generation. PowerPoint presentation creation + outline. Outlook email + summarization. Teams meeting summaries + action items. The 5 anchor use cases.
**Sources:** [4].

### Chapter 3 — The licensing + procurement decision (~5 min)
**What:** $30/user/month — who gets it, when, why. Build a quantified business case. Active-user fraction matters more than license count. Pilot → scale → enterprise rollout pattern.
**Sources:** [2], [3], [18], [19].

### Chapter 4 — Security + compliance posture (~5 min)
**What:** Tenant isolation. Microsoft Graph integration with permissions inheritance. Microsoft Purview for DLP. Microsoft Defender for Cloud Apps. EU AI Act + HIPAA + DPDPA. The IT-admin posture document.
**Sources:** [6], [11], [12], [13].

### Chapter 5 — Adoption + change management (~5 min)
**What:** Active-user gap is the central problem. The 3 moves: model the behaviour, make the new way easier, track adoption. Adoption squads / champions network. Office hours pattern.
**Sources:** [14], [17], [19].

### Chapter 6 — Copilot Studio + agentic extensions (~5 min)
**What:** Microsoft Copilot Studio for custom agents. When to extend Copilot vs use it as-is. Integration with Microsoft Graph + Power Platform. The build vs configure decision.
**Sources:** [5].

### Chapter 7 — Measuring real outcomes (~5 min)
**What:** Tetlock calibration on Microsoft's ~30% number. What active-user means. How to measure your own deployment honestly. Microsoft Adoption Score + Viva Insights + analytical platforms.
**Sources:** [2], [16], [18].

### Chapter 8 — Making it stick · your Copilot rollout roadmap (~4 min)
**What:** Pick the use cases. Pick the pilot team. Pick the change-management cadence. Trust trip-wires: sensitive-data classification before deployment, no Copilot without compliance posture, active-user fraction tracked monthly, no "set and forget."
**Sources:** Composite.
