The four-pillar governance every AI program needs.

*Pillar one — scope.* Which AI systems are in scope. Which are out. *Documented.* Updated quarterly.

*Pillar two — risk classification.* For each in-scope system, what's the risk tier. *Use the EU AI Act framing — minimal, limited, high-risk, prohibited.* Even if you don't operate in the EU. It's the common language.

*Pillar three — monitoring.* What are you measuring. Bias drift. Accuracy degradation. Customer complaints. *Reported to the AI council.*

*Pillar four — audit.* Internal audit. External audit. Documentation that can withstand both.
