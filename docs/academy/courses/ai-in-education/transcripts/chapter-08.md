Four trust trip-wires. *Do not cross.*

*Trip-wire three — surveillance over support.* The moment AI in education becomes primarily monitoring students, trust collapses.

*Trip-wire four — privacy theatre.* Signing vendor contracts without the 3 essentials, then telling parents data is protected. *Don't do it.*
