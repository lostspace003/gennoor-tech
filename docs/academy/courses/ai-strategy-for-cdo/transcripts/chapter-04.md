> [S2 ▸ reveal 1 · the starvation problem]

The starvation problem.

Without explicit sequencing, two patterns emerge.

*Pattern one — AI starves data.* CFO sees AI excitement. Funds AI platforms. Underfunds data plumbing. *Six quarters later, AI features stall because the data underneath isn't production-grade.*

*Pattern two — data starves AI.* CDO advocates for foundational data work. Funds catalog, MDM, lineage. AI program waits in line. *Two years later, the board asks where AI value is. Foundation work is impressive in slide decks, invisible in the P&L.*

Both patterns are common. *The cure is explicit allocation, defended at finance committee.*

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:15]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 3-horizon allocation]

The three-horizon allocation framework.

*Horizon 1 — ship now (12 months).* AI features in current quarter or next. Data work that directly enables them. *50-60% of CDO budget.*

*Horizon 2 — enable next (12-24 months).* AI use cases identified but not yet in development. Data foundations they will need. *25-35% of budget.*

*Horizon 3 — foundational (24+ months).* The MDM consolidation. The full catalog. Comprehensive lineage. *10-15% of budget.*

The allocation discipline. *Defend each horizon to your CFO with a specific outcome at that horizon's exit point.* Vague horizon-3 spending is the easiest to cut. Don't make it easy.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · CFO-defensible framing]

The CFO-defensible framing.

CFOs don't fund "data quality." They fund outcomes. *Translate.*

*Don't say "we need data catalog investment."* Say "we need to ship 5 AI features in H2. Currently engineers spend 40% of cycle time looking for data. The catalog reduces that to 15%. Year-over-year ROI is X."

*Don't say "we need MDM consolidation."* Say "Customer 360 AI features can't ship until master data is consolidated across CRM and ERP. Consolidation is 8 months. Without it, 3 of our 12 planned AI features push to next FY."

CFOs fund concrete outcomes. *Always translate data work into AI feature unblocking.*
