The failure mode most AI governance committees hit.

Month one. *Excitement. Charter signed. All three roles enthusiastic.*

Month four. *Friction. CISO blocks a deployment. CDO escalates. CRO is unsure who owns the call.*

Month six. *The committee meets every two weeks and approves nothing.* AI program slows. Business units route around the committee. *Shadow AI begins.*

The cause. *RACI was nominal, not specific.* Same problem we've seen with every governance committee since SOX.

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:15]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 3-pillar RACI]

The three-pillar RACI that holds.

*Pillar one — data quality + lineage.* CDO is Responsible. CRO is Consulted on regulatory implications. CISO is Informed.

*Pillar two — security + access controls.* CISO is Responsible. CDO is Consulted on data classification. CRO is Consulted on risk acceptance.

*Pillar three — model + AI lifecycle risk.* CRO is Responsible (the risk function owns model risk). CDO is Consulted on data inputs. CISO is Consulted on attack surface.

Each pillar has one R, not three. *That's the discipline.* If two execs both think they're R, the committee freezes.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · 3 turf fights]

Three turf fights to pre-empt explicitly in the charter.

*Fight one — who approves a new external AI vendor?* Answer: CISO approves security posture. CDO approves data-handling clauses. CRO approves model risk classification. *Each in their lane, parallel review, no sequential blocking.*

*Fight two — who owns AI incident response?* Answer: CRO owns the incident response framework. CISO owns technical containment. CDO owns data-impact assessment. *Pre-agreed playbook.*

*Fight three — who reports to the board?* Answer: CRO presents model risk. CDO presents AI value + KPIs. CISO presents AI security posture. *Three voices, agreed before the meeting.*

> [S5 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 3:30]

> [S5 ▸ reveal 1 · honest scoring]

How to score governance partnership. *Two questions.*

*Question one — does your AI governance committee have a documented 3-pillar RACI with one R per pillar?* Yes specific green · "we have a charter" amber · *no/multiple Rs red — committee will freeze*.

*Question two — has the committee approved at least 5 things in the last quarter, or has it been a blocker?* >5 approved green · 2-4 amber · *<2 red — committee is theatre*.
