> [S2 ▸ reveal 1 · 3 phases]

The three portfolio phases.

*Phase one — pilot.* Small. Tested. Funded for six to twelve weeks. *Outcome: keep, kill, or scale decision.*

*Phase two — scale.* The pilot worked at small scale. Expand to one full business unit or geography. *Outcome: operational impact at meaningful scale.*

*Phase three — embed.* Scale worked. Now embedded in the operating model. *Production-grade. Operating cost. Capability baked in.*

Most enterprises in 2026 — *too many pilots, not enough scaled, almost nothing embedded.* The portfolio is top-heavy. Action: kill some pilots. *Concentrate resources on the scale phase.*

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:15]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · the pyramid pattern]

The pyramid pattern. *Healthy AI portfolio shape.*

At the base — *ten to fifteen pilots running.* Each lean. Each focused. Each on a 90-day cycle.

In the middle — *three to five scaled initiatives.* Funded for a year. Owned by a business unit. Measured on operational outcomes.

At the top — *one or two embedded capabilities.* Multi-year. Producing recurring value. *The compounding payoff.*

That's the shape that works. *Most orgs have the inverse — fifty pilots, two scaled, zero embedded.* Inversion is the problem.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · the funnel discipline]

The funnel discipline. *Movement between phases is the metric.*

*Pilot to scale.* In a healthy portfolio — twenty to thirty percent of pilots make it to scale. *Most should die. That's the system working.*

*Scale to embed.* Fifty to seventy percent of scaled initiatives make it to embedded. *Once it works at scale, embedding is the natural next step.*

*Embedded — multi-year.* Stays in the operating model. Maintained by ops. *Continues delivering.*

If your funnel doesn't flow — pilots stay in pilot, scaled stay in scale — *the discipline is broken. Fix that before adding more initiatives.*

> [S5 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 3:30]

> [S5 ▸ reveal 1 · honest scoring]

How to score portfolio shape honestly. *Two questions.*

*Question one — what's your current phase distribution?* If pilot count > 10x scale count, your portfolio is top-heavy. *Action: kill pilots, concentrate.*

*Question two — what's your pilot-to-scale conversion rate in the last twelve months?* Above twenty percent is green. Five to twenty percent is amber. *Under five percent is red.*

> [S5 ▸ reveal 2 · the close]

The Monday move. *Map your AI portfolio onto the three phases.* If the shape is inverted — that's your highest-leverage portfolio conversation this quarter.
