## 5 readiness dimensions

1. **Data** — quality, lineage, accessibility, governance
2. **Talent** — AI/ML skills, data literacy across functions, leadership AI fluency
3. **Governance** — NIST AI RMF + EU AI Act readiness, risk register, model lifecycle
4. **Infrastructure** — compute, MLOps, model registry, observability
5. **Leadership commitment** — exec sponsorship, funded roadmap, ROI patience
