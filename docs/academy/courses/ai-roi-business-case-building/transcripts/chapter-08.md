Day five. *Pre-walk with each approver before formal submission.*

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · 5 trust trip-wires]

Five trust trip-wires. *Do not cross.*

*Trip-wire one — vendor-supplied ROI used directly.* Always recalculate with your own assumptions.

*Trip-wire two — costs limited to year one.* Always model year two and three.

*Trip-wire three — risk register empty or generic.* Always name 5 AI-specific risks with mitigations.

*Trip-wire four — single benefit number, no scenarios.* Always model three scenarios.

*Trip-wire five — baseline not captured before launch.* Without it, ROI realisation is impossible.
