Chapter six. *Stakeholder maps.* Who needs to say yes. Who needs to not say no.

> [S2 ▸ reveal 1 · 3 stakeholder categories]

Three stakeholder categories.

*Category one — sponsors.* The executive who needs the outcome. *They drive yes.*

*Category two — approvers.* CFO, finance, IT, security, procurement, legal. *They can stop the project. Their no kills it.*

*Category three — influencers.* Adjacent functions, affected teams, technical reviewers. *They shape perception but rarely vote.*

Map each one. *By name, not by role.* Position in the org. Stake in the outcome. Concern profile.

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:15]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 4 CFO questions]

Four questions your CFO will ask.

*Question one — what's the payback?* Have it ready. Three years or under usually clears. Over five usually doesn't.

*Question two — what's the worst case?* Don't hide it. Lead with the worst case and explain why the base case is realistic.

*Question three — what could derail this?* The five named risks. Name them before the CFO does.

*Question four — what does success look like by month 12?* Specific. Measurable. Auditable.

If you can answer all four in under three minutes — you're CFO-ready.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · pre-mortem]

The pre-mortem discipline.

Before submitting the business case to approval. *Run a pre-mortem.*

Imagine it's eighteen months in. The project has failed. *What went wrong?*

The team brainstorms causes. List them. Group them. *Most causes will map to one of your named risks. The ones that don't are blind spots.*

For each blind spot — either add to risk register or strengthen the case to address.

*Pre-mortems take 90 minutes. They save business cases.*

How to score stakeholder readiness honestly. *Two questions.*

*Question one — every approver has been pre-walked through the case before formal review?* Yes for all green · most amber · *some/none red — surprise in the meeting.*

*Question two — pre-mortem run and documented?* Yes green · informally amber · *no red.*
