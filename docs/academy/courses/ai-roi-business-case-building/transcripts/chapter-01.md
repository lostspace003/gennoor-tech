The principle.

*Most AI business cases fail not because the AI doesn't work — but because the case is built wrong.* Inflated benefits. Missing costs. Risk treated as zero. *CFO and finance team see the gap, and the project doesn't get funded.*

Teams that get funding build cases that survive finance scrutiny. *Realistic benefits. Honest costs. Named risks. Defensible methodology.*

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:15]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 4 fail modes]

Four failure modes of typical AI business cases.

*Mode one — vendor-supplied ROI.* Vendor says "60% productivity improvement." *Methodology unclear. Comparable peers anonymous. CFO can't validate.* Project paused.

*Mode two — benefits doubled.* Same benefit counted twice — once as "time saved" and once as "cost reduction." *Finance catches this.*

*Mode three — costs missing.* Implementation costs included. *Change management, data prep, retraining cycles, ongoing license drift — missing.*

*Mode four — risk treated as zero.* Project plan assumes everything goes right. *No scenario for delay. No scenario for model performance below target.*

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · 6-component framework]

The six-component business case framework.

*Component one — problem statement.* What problem in business terms, not AI terms.

*Component two — benefits.* Direct and indirect. Quantified honestly.

*Component three — costs.* Visible and hidden. Year one through year three.

*Component four — risk register.* Three to five named risks with mitigations.

*Component five — financial metric.* NPV, IRR, payback chosen deliberately.

*Component six — stakeholder map.* Who needs to say yes. Who needs to not say no.

Every one of these missing means the business case is incomplete.

> [S5 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 3:30]

> [S5 ▸ reveal 1 · honest scoring]

How to score your business case readiness honestly. *Two questions.*

*Question one — has finance reviewed methodology?* Yes and approved green · reviewed not approved amber · *not yet shown red — finance will reject when they see it.*

*Question two — does the case withstand a 30% benefit haircut and still pass?* Yes green · marginally amber · *no red — case is too optimistic.*
