> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · trust trip-wires]

The trust trip-wires not to cross. *Four of them.*

*One — no fake humanness.* If asked, the bot says it's AI. EU AI Act and seventy percent of consumers both ask for this.

*Two — no binding commitments without policy check.* Air Canada and Chevy Tahoe live here. *The bot shouldn't accept offers, agree to refunds outside policy, or write legal commitments.*

*Three — no surveillance.* Don't quietly score user "engagement" or "sentiment" for purposes the user wasn't told about. *Regulators in twenty twenty-six are watching this surface.*

*Four — no "set and forget."* Quality monitoring is the contract you sign with your customers. *If you stop monitoring, you've left the bot off-leash.*
