> [S2 ▸ reveal 1 · the principle]

The principle that runs through this course. *Use AI for volume. Use humans for value.*

Customer service is the function where AI is most visible to customers — and most visibly disappointing when it goes wrong. *Air Canada Moffatt. NYC MyCity. Klarna\'s public reversal.* Every CS-AI failure becomes a media story.

The teams that get this right use AI to scale volume work — basic inquiries, status checks, simple triage. *And they keep humans in the loop for value moments — distressed customers, complex issues, anything where the relationship matters.* The discipline is knowing which is which.

> [S3 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 1:00]

> [S3 ▸ reveal 1 · 6 plays that ship]

Six customer-service AI plays that consistently ship value.

*Play one — agent assist.* AI as the agent\'s sidekick. *Drafting responses. Surfacing knowledge. Summarising history. Augmenting the human, not replacing them.*

*Play two — scoped deflection bots.* Narrow-scope bots for high-frequency, low-stakes queries. Order status. Returns process. Hours of operation. *Strong escalation paths to humans.*

*Play three — multilingual support.* Real-time translation. Multilingual knowledge bases. Critical for global organisations and inclusive service.

*Play four — sentiment + escalation detection.* AI surfaces customers who are frustrated or at risk of churning. Routes them to senior agents.

*Play five — quality assurance at scale.* AI samples and scores agent interactions. Surfaces coaching opportunities.

*Play six — voice support augmentation.* Assist patterns for call centres. Real-time agent prompting. Post-call summarisation.

> [S4 ▸ slide change · t ≈ 2:30]

> [S4 ▸ reveal 1 · 3 anti-plays]

Three CS-AI anti-plays.

*Anti-play one — full-bot replacement of human support.* Sounds attractive in budget meetings. *Loses customer lifetime value within months.* Klarna reversed course publicly. So have others.

*Anti-play two — chatbots without strong grounding.* Air Canada Moffatt cross-domain — chatbot misrepresentation became company liability. *NYC MyCity gave illegal advice to citizens.* Grounding is not optional.

*Anti-play three — AI gate-keeping access to humans.* When customers cannot reach a human after reasonable effort, they leave. *Or sue. Or post the screenshot. All three damage the brand.*
