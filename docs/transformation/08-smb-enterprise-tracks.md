# 08 — SMB vs Enterprise Tracks

## Why two tracks

A 30-person business in Karachi and a 30,000-person bank in Dubai have **the same problem** (figure out AI), but they need **different buying experiences**:

- The SMB wants packaged, fixed-price, fast, low-friction.
- The Enterprise wants strategic, custom, governed, procurement-friendly.

Both deserve to feel like the site was built for them. Today's site speaks 90% enterprise. The new site speaks both — at the same level of polish.

---

## The SMB Track

### Who this is for
- Companies with 10–250 employees
- Founders, owner-operators, COOs, Heads of Ops
- Annual revenue typically $1M–$50M
- Decision-makers buy in days, not quarters

### What they need to hear
- *"Will this work for me, at my size?"*
- *"What does it cost?"* (precise, not "let's chat")
- *"How fast?"*
- *"Do I need a tech team?"* (no)
- *"Can I see something working before I commit?"* (yes — the diagnostic, free)

### The 5 SMB packages

#### S1 — AI Readiness Kit ($3k–$5k, 1 week)
A productized version of Phase 1 (Diagnose).
- 2 stakeholder interviews
- Lightweight data audit
- 5-use-case backlog with ROI scores
- 12-month AI roadmap (3-5 pages)
- 60-min readout call
- Output: PDF report your leadership team can act on

#### S2 — Copilot Kickstart ($10k–$15k, 4 weeks)
A productized Phase 2 + Phase 3 hybrid focused on Microsoft 365 Copilot.
- Licensing strategy
- Persona-based prompt libraries for 3 roles
- 2-day live workshop (up to 15 people)
- Pilot with 1 team for 3 weeks
- Adoption metrics dashboard
- Output: a Copilot rollout playbook + a measurably-adopting pilot team

#### S3 — Single Agent Build ($25k–$40k, 6 weeks)
One PoC from the catalog, delivered end-to-end.
- 1 week scoping
- 4 weeks build
- 1 week handover + hypercare
- Client owns Azure tenant + code
- Output: a deployed agent in your environment

#### S4 — Team AI Upskill ($5k–$12k, 2 weeks)
Productized Phase 2 for non-technical teams.
- 2-day workshop OR 4 half-day virtual sessions
- Up to 25 people
- Function-specific (Finance OR Sales OR HR — pick one)
- Includes paired PDF course pre-read
- Output: a trained team with prompt libraries and a 30-day adoption plan

#### S5 — SMB Sustain ($2k–$4k/month, ongoing)
Phase 5 for businesses with a deployed system.
- Quarterly health check
- Monthly 2-hour office hours
- Always-on async Slack
- Output: keeps your AI working

### The SMB landing page
Located at `/solutions/for-smb`. Wireframe per [04-page-blueprints.md](04-page-blueprints.md). Critical content rules:
- **Prices shown.** SMBs hate "request a quote" — they bounce.
- **Timelines shown.** Days and weeks, not "depending on scope."
- **WhatsApp button visible.** Many SMBs prefer messaging to email forms. (Per [feedback_form_fields.md](../../../memory/feedback_form_fields.md).)
- **One success story** for an SMB-sized client (real or representative composite).
- **No founder hero.** SMBs don't care about MCT certs. They care about "will it work for me."

### SMB sales motion
- **Entry:** PDF course download → email nurture → diagnostic offer
- **Path 1 (fast):** WhatsApp/contact form → 30-min call → S1 or S4 sold same week
- **Path 2 (slower):** newsletter → workshop → S2 → S3 → S5
- **No discovery rate-card.** Discovery call is always free.
- **Proposal turnaround:** 5 working days, max.

## The Enterprise Track

### Who this is for
- Organizations with 250+ employees
- CIOs, CDOs, CTOs, CHROs, Heads of Transformation
- Regulated, multinational, multi-stakeholder
- Decision-makers buy across quarters, with procurement involved

### What they need to hear
- *"Have you done this before, at our scale, in our region, in our sector?"*
- *"How do you handle governance, IP, data residency, compliance?"*
- *"Who is on the team — by role and seniority?"*
- *"What's your delivery cadence?"*
- *"Can our SI/internal team work alongside you?"*

### The Enterprise engagement model

Not packages. Programs.

#### E1 — Strategic Diagnostic ($25k–$80k, 4 weeks)
Phase 1 done deeply.
- Multi-stakeholder workshops
- Data + tech + process + people audit
- 20–40 use case backlog
- 18-month AI roadmap with capital plan
- Governance & risk charter
- Executive offsite facilitation
- Output: a board-ready transformation plan

#### E2 — Executive & Functional Bootcamps ($30k–$150k per program)
Phase 2 at scale.
- C-suite bootcamps (3–10 days)
- Functional cohorts (HR, Finance, Ops — separate tracks)
- Technical guild track
- Custom curriculum with client data scenarios
- Adoption playbooks per function
- Output: an AI-literate organization across levels

#### E3 — Strategic Pilot ($50k–$180k, 6–8 weeks)
Phase 3 with executive sponsorship.
- One use case taken from backlog
- Multi-team coordination
- Production-grade architecture from day one
- Compliance & risk review
- Weekly steering committee
- Go/no-go business case for Phase 4
- Output: a working system with sign-off path to scale

#### E4 — Transformation Program ($150k–$600k+, 9–20 weeks)
Phase 4 at full Enterprise scale.
- Multi-use-case scaled from pilots
- CoE / Hub-and-spoke org design
- Production deployment(s)
- CI/CD, MLOps, evaluation, observability
- Adoption + change management
- Internal team co-build (knowledge transfer is contractual)
- Output: scaled, governed AI capability inside the org

#### E5 — Enterprise Sustain ($15k–$60k/month)
Phase 5 retainer at scale.
- Quarterly health checks across all deployed systems
- Monthly steering reviews
- Continuous L&D refresh
- Annual strategy day
- New use case incubation pipeline

#### E6 — AI Center of Excellence (CoE) Setup ($80k–$250k)
A standalone Enterprise offer.
- Charter & operating model
- Role design + hiring profiles
- Tool, governance, and process setup
- 90-day rollout plan with us embedded
- Output: a functioning internal AI CoE that the client owns

### The Enterprise landing page
Located at `/solutions/for-enterprise`. Wireframe per [04](04-page-blueprints.md). Critical content rules:
- **Named case studies.** MCIT Saudi, etc.

### Enterprise sales motion
- **Entry:** white paper / case study download → BDR-style outreach → C-level intro call
- **Path:** Diagnostic (E1) → Pilot (E3) → Program (E4) → Sustain (E5)
- **Discovery is paid** at the Diagnostic stage — but exploratory calls are free.
- **Proposal turnaround:** 10 working days, with a presentation.
- **References available** after scoping conversation.

## Pricing display strategy

The website **must show price bands**. Two reasons:

1. **Trust** — hidden pricing is a 2010s tactic that signals you'll quote based on the buyer's wallet. Modern buyers (especially SMBs) see this and bounce.
2. **Self-qualification** — buyers under our SMB band don't waste our time; buyers over the Enterprise band understand the value before they call.

**How we display it:**

- **SMB packages:** point ranges. *"$10k–$15k."*
- **Enterprise engagements:** wider ranges. *"$50k–$180k depending on scope."*
- **Sustain retainers:** monthly ranges. *"$2k–$40k/mo."*
- **Custom builds:** *"scoped after 30-min discovery; typical projects $30k–$250k."*

Yes, this is unusual. Yes, it makes us look like we're not in the same conversation as Accenture. **That's the point.** We're the firm a CIO loves *because* we're not the consulting black box.

## How the SMB and Enterprise tracks share infrastructure

Both tracks use:
- The same **Gennoor Way** (just compressed for SMB, expanded for Enterprise)
- The same **PoC catalog** (same PoCs, different price bands)
- The same **course PDFs** (free for both; pilots different)
- The same **diagnostic tool** (`/ai-readiness`)
- The same **founder/team** (just more layers for Enterprise)

What's different:
- **Selling motion** (SMB = fast, self-serve; Enterprise = consultative)
- **Procurement surface** (SMB = card on file or invoice; Enterprise = MSA + PO)
- **Sustain depth** (SMB = light retainer; Enterprise = multi-stream)
- **Documentation depth** (SMB = brief; Enterprise = governance-grade)

This is the most efficient possible two-audience operation — same core, different shell.
