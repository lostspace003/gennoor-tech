- Above-the-fold mentions zero founder credentials. They appear in position 7-8.

- "Outcomes strip" numbers must all be real (already true on the site).

## `/the-gennoor-way` (new page)

The most-linked-to page in the site. This is where SEO, sales, and education converge.

**Structure:**

1. Hero: the 5-phase diagram, full-width.
2. **One sentence per phase**, with anchor links.
3. **"Which phase are you in?"** — 6 yes/no questions → recommends a phase.
4. **Detailed phase breakdowns** — for each phase: goal, inputs, outputs, time, price band, who buys it.
5. **Entry-point matrix** — visual showing which phase to enter from.
6. **Three example journeys:**
   - "SMB starting from zero" → full 5 phases over 9 months
   - "Enterprise with strategy already" → entering at Phase 3
   - "Live system that's drifting" → entering at Phase 5
7. **FAQ** — 10 questions: "Can I just buy phase 2? What if I already have a vendor? Who owns the IP? Do you work on AWS too?" etc.
8. **CTA**: "Run the diagnostic" + "Book a scoping call"

This page should be 2,000+ words. It is a *pillar page* for SEO around "AI transformation framework" and "enterprise AI adoption."

## `/about/team-and-delivery` (NEW)

This is the missing page that converts enterprise buyers. They need to know:
- Who actually shows up to do the work
- How many people
- What's their seniority
- What's the delivery model
- What's the org's continuity (founder hit by a bus = ?)

Even if today's team is small, this page should say so honestly — "boutique, senior-only delivery" is a *feature*, not a weakness. Show the role mix (Solutions Architect, Senior Trainer, Engagement Manager, PoC Engineer) — even if Jalal is wearing 3 of those hats today.
