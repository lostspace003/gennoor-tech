Course Overview

This 5-day hands-on bootcamp takes participants from understanding generative AI fundamentals to building enterprise-grade AI workflows using Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and no-code agent platforms — designed for both business users and IT professionals.

Two tracks in one bootcamp. Business users learn to leverage AI tools for productivity, analysis, and decision-making. IT professionals go deeper into prompt engineering, Copilot Studio customization, and AI agent development. Both tracks share common sessions and collaborate on real-world scenarios.

Learning Outcomes

1
Master prompt engineering — write effective prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot across business scenarios
2
Use Microsoft 365 Copilot — automate workflows in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams with AI assistance
3
Build no-code AI agents — design and deploy custom Copilot Studio agents for customer service, HR, and IT support
4

5
Apply AI to real work — document analysis, data extraction, report generation, email drafting, meeting summarization, code assistance
6
Navigate responsible AI — handle hallucinations, data privacy, prompt injection risks, and enterprise compliance
