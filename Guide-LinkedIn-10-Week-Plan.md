## POSTING STRATEGY & BEST PRACTICES

### Where to Post
- **Post from your PERSONAL profile** (Jalal Ahmed Khan), NOT the Gennoor Tech company page
- Personal profiles get 5-10x more organic reach than company pages on LinkedIn
- Share/repost select posts TO the Gennoor Tech company page after posting on your personal profile

### Posting Schedule
- **Monday** — Start-of-week insight (catch professionals during morning scroll)
- **Wednesday** — Mid-week deep dive or blog promotion (peak LinkedIn engagement day)
- **Friday** — Lighter/conversational post — polls, stories, predictions (end-of-week reflection)

### Posting Times (Optimal for India + Saudi Arabia + Global)
- **8:00-9:00 AM IST** (10:30 AM Saudi / 2:30 AM EST) — catches India morning + Saudi mid-morning
- Alternative: **12:00-1:00 PM IST** for broader global reach

### Engagement Rules
1. Reply to EVERY comment within the first 2 hours (this is critical for the algorithm)
2. After posting, immediately comment on 3-5 other posts in your feed to boost visibility
3. Never edit a post within the first hour — it kills reach
4. Tag people or companies ONLY when genuinely relevant (not for engagement bait)
5. When sharing a blog link, put the URL in the FIRST COMMENT, not in the post body (LinkedIn suppresses posts with external links)
6. Add a line at the end like "Link in first comment" or "Full guide at gennoor.com (link in comments)"

### Content Mix Over 10 Weeks
- 10 Blog Promotion posts (link to specific articles)
- 6 Insight/Lesson posts (share experience from 14+ years)
- 5 Industry Trend posts (position as thought leader)
- 4 Personal Story/Authority posts (certifications, client wins)
- 3 Question/Poll posts (engagement drivers)
- 2 Contrarian/Hot Take posts (algorithm fuel)
