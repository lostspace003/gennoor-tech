Welcome to AB-100

Two things to set expectations before we begin. What this course is — and what it deliberately isn't.

WHAT IT IS

Architecture & design judgment

Real-world preparation for designing, governing, and shipping AI-powered business solutions on the Microsoft platform.

  • Decision frameworks & trade-off analysis
  • Scenario-based learning across 10 enterprise scenarios
  • End-to-end coverage — plan, design, deploy
  • Aligned to AB-100 exam objectives
WHAT IT ISN'T

Not configuration training

This course assumes you can configure tools. Our focus is the architecture around the configuration.

  • Not click-by-click tool tutorials
  • Not hands-on labs (no exercises)
  • Not test-prep memorization
  • Not for first-time AI users
