# Google Business Profile — Verification & Optimization Guide

## Step 1: Confirm Your Profile Is Live

1. Go to **https://business.google.com**
2. Sign in with the Google account you used to create the profile
3. You should see **Gennoor Tech** listed as your business
4. Check the **Verification status**:
   - **Verified** (green checkmark) = You're live on Google
   - **Pending verification** = Google is reviewing (wait 3-7 days)
   - **Not verified** = You need to complete verification

**Quick test:** Google search for `"Gennoor Tech"` — if your Business Profile shows on the right side of search results, it's working.

## Step 3: Get Your First 5 Reviews

Reviews are critical for local SEO and credibility. Here's how:

### Create Your Review Link
1. In Google Business Profile, go to **Home**
2. Look for **"Get more reviews"** card → click **"Share review form"**
3. Copy the short link (looks like `https://g.page/r/XXXXX/review`)
4. Save this link — you'll send it to clients

### Ask 5-10 Past Clients
Send this message (personalize for each client):

```
Subject: Quick favor — 30 seconds

Hi [Name],

I hope you're doing well. I recently set up a Google Business Profile for
Gennoor Tech and would really appreciate a short review from you.

If you could spare 30 seconds, here's the direct link:
[Your review link]

A few lines about your experience with our training/consulting would mean
a lot. Thank you!

Best regards,
Jalal
```

### What to Ask Them to Mention
- The specific service (AI training, consulting, PoC)
- Their role/company (if comfortable)
- A specific outcome or result
- Keywords naturally: "AI training", "Azure", "Copilot", "enterprise"

## Step 4: Post Regular Updates

Google Business Profile has a **Posts** feature — use it:

1. Go to your profile → **Add update**
2. Post types:
   - **What's new:** Share when you publish a new blog post
   - **Event:** Promote upcoming webinars
   - **Offer:** Promote free AI Readiness Assessment

**Post weekly** — even a simple "New blog post: [title] — Read it at gennoor.com/resources/blog/[slug]" with an image keeps your profile active.

## Step 5: Verify Everything Is Working

### Check 1: Google Search
- Search `"Gennoor Tech"` on Google
- Your Business Profile should appear on the right (desktop) or top (mobile)
- It should show: name, category, website link, phone, reviews

### Check 2: Google Maps
- Go to **maps.google.com**
- Search `"Gennoor Tech"`
- Your business should appear (if you added an address or service area)

### Check 3: Profile Completeness
- In Business Profile dashboard, look for the **"Complete your profile"** card
- It shows a percentage — aim for **100%**

### Check 4: Insights
- Go to **Performance** in your Business Profile
- After 1-2 weeks, you'll see:
  - How many people viewed your profile
  - How many clicked your website link
  - How many requested directions
  - What search terms triggered your profile

3. Respond to any new reviews (always respond, even to positive ones)
4. Update photos if you have new ones
5. Check for and answer any **Questions** people post on your profile
