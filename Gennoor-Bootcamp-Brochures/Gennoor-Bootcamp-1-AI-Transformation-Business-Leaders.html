AI Transformation for Business Leaders

Course Overview

This 5-day executive bootcamp equips business leaders with the strategic understanding, practical vocabulary, and hands-on experience to drive AI transformation in their organizations — without writing a single line of code.

This is not a technical course. It's a strategic leadership program designed for executives who need to make AI decisions — not build AI systems. You'll leave with a clear AI roadmap, ROI framework, and the confidence to lead your organization's AI journey.

Learning Outcomes

1
Understand the AI landscape — distinguish between Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Agentic AI with clarity
2
Evaluate AI opportunities — identify high-impact use cases in your industry using structured discovery frameworks
3
Build business cases for AI — create ROI models, risk assessments, and stakeholder alignment strategies
4
Use AI tools hands-on — experience Microsoft 365 Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot Studio through live demos
5
Lead AI transformation — draft your organization's AI adoption roadmap with governance, ethics, and change management built in
6
Navigate responsible AI — understand bias, privacy, compliance, and responsible AI principles for enterprise deployment
