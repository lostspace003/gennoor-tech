## ✅ Posting checklist

- **Upload the .mp4 directly** to LinkedIn (native video gets far more reach than a YouTube link).
- **Format:** portrait 720×1560 (9:19.5) — fills mobile screens. ✔ already exported.
- **Sound on:** the value is in the narration + recitation. Add a line like *"🔊 sound on"* at the top, since LinkedIn autoplays muted.
- **Hook in line 1:** the first sentence is all most people see before "…more" — keep it strong.
- **Put the link in the first comment**, not the post body (links in-body suppress reach).
- **Best time:** Tue–Thu, mid-morning your audience's timezone.
- **Add the alt text** (above) for accessibility and a small reach bump.
- Consider posting the **51s English cut** instead if your audience skews international — shorter videos hold completion rate.
